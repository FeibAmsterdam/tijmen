Keybindings
===========
Draft!

Movement & Aim
--------------
Player movement: WASD
Gun/Util aim: Mouse

Weapons
-------
Shoot: MouseButton1
Cycle weapons: MouseWheel Up/Down
Selection weapon: 1,2,3,4,5...
Reload: R

Grapple Hook
------------
Shoot hook: MouseButton2

Portals
-------
Shoot portal #1: Q
Shoot portal #2: E

Misc
----
Use: F

GUI
---
Pause/Bossmode: Tab (then Q to quit the game)
Back to previous menu/level: Esc
